Project #1:

	Name: Weather Station

	Purpose: To collect the temperature and humidity of the air around it and then display this.

	Components: Arduino Uno, USB cord to connect Arduino to PC, DHT11 temperature and humidity sensor, LCD, LCD Adaptor, Bread board, 7 hole-to-hole wires, 9 pin-to-pin wires

	Wiring: Refer to "Complete Setup.jpg" for wiring directions

	- Libraries used for source code in "IDE Libraries" folder
